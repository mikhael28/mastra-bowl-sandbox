## Summary

- Official pricing model: Zoho CRM offers two user levels— Organization (org) users with full CRM access and Team users with access limited to team modules. Team users cannot own records or access admin features, reports/dashboards, automation, AI features, or certain integrations; they can operate within team modules and perform events like emails and custom views within those modules.

- Editions and cost structure:
  - Free tier: Promises 100% free forever for basic or “free for 3 users” usage (likely team users; check exact limits on the page).
  - Paid plans: Standard plan highlighted (beginner-friendly) with typical features like workflows & assignment rules, AI agents, cadences, reports & dashboards, and sales forecasting. Pricing shown in multiple currencies (USD, INR, EUR, GBP, JPY, SGD, AED, ARS, MXN, NZD, INR, CAD). The page emphasizes annual vs monthly pricing options with potential savings for annual billing (up to 34%).
  - Notable: The page lists an ongoing emphasis on “team users” in the CRM For Everyone UI, with notes about upgrading from legacy to the new UI for team features.

- API quotas and automation/workflow limits:
  - The page outlines that team users have restricted capabilities (no admin rights, no reports/dashboards, no automation ownership, etc.). It implies quotas and permissions differ between org vs team modules, but it does not provide explicit API request quotas, rate limits, or exact workflow execution limits on the public page.

- Custom modules/objects:
  - The page discusses org vs team modules and permissions but does not detail custom module/object creation quotas or limits. Team modules allow owning and performing operations within those modules, with restrictions on cross-module automation and reporting.

- SMB vs mid-market pricing:
  - The page focuses on standard/beginner-friendly features and provides pricing in multiple currencies, but it does not explicitly segment SMB vs mid-market pricing tiers. The presence of a standard plan with essential features suggests an entry-level target audience.

- Third-party analyses:
  - The official page links to licensing guides for team users and notes that team features exist in the new UI; for detailed quotas, API limits, or licensing nuances, refer to the linked “licensing guide” PDF and Zoho CRM API documentation or official pricing FAQ.
