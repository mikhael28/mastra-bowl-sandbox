## Summary

Summary tailored to your query:

HubSpot’s AI in workflows introduces Breeze, an AI-enabled assistant that helps build enrollment triggers and workflow actions. To use AI features, enable AI in your account settings (Account management > AI, then Access tab) and configure what data is shared. HubSpot emphasizes transparency and security via AI Trust FAQs and AI Model Cards, detailing data use and compliance. Availability spans Marketing, Sales, Service, Data, Smart CRM, and Commerce hubs at Professional and Enterprise levels.

Key patterns and considerations for integration:
- APIs and webhooks: AI-enabled workflows can trigger actions and enrollments, suggesting a pattern of using APIs/webhooks to instantiate AI-driven decisions within automation.
- Custom objects: Potential use of custom objects to store AI-processed data and drive workflow logic.
- Email sync: AI-driven workflow actions may leverage email data for context and personalization.
- Pros for SMB vs mid-market: SMBs benefit from streamlined, AI-assisted automation with lower manual overhead, while mid-market may leverage more complex, scalable AI workflows and richer data integrations; consider data governance and security controls accordingly.
- Security/compliance: Review AI Trust FAQs and AI Model Cards to understand data sharing, model behavior, and compliance requirements before enabling AI in production.

Practical steps:
- Turn on AI in your HubSpot settings (Settings > AI in Account management).
- Configure data-sharing preferences in AI settings to align with your data governance.
- Design workflows using Breeze to set enrollment triggers and AI-driven actions, integrating with APIs, webhooks, and (where appropriate) custom objects.
- Monitor AI behavior via Trust FAQs and Model Cards, adjusting settings as needed.
