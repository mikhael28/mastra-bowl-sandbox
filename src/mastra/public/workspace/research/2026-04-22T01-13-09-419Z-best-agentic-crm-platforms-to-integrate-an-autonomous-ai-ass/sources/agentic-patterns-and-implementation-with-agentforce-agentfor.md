Summary:

- This Salesforce Architect whitepaper examines agentic patterns and how to implement autonomous AI agents on the Salesforce Agentforce platform, with practical guidance for building agent-powered CRM solutions.
- Key agent types covered:
  - Conversational agents: reactive, NLP-driven interfaces that handle well-defined tasks and resolve user intent efficiently.
  - Proactive agents: event-driven agents that act without prompts, triggering workflows when data changes or conditions are met.
  - Ambient agents: contextually aware background agents that operate in the environment to monitor signals.
  - Autonomous and collaborative agents: capable of independent reasoning and multi-agent coordination (A2A) using standardized protocols.

- Considerations for SMB and mid-market deployments:
