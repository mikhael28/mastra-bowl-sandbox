- Enterprise is a custom, negotiated plan rather than a self-serve per-seat option.
- It targets teams needing advanced hosting (including hybrid or self-hosted options), custom SSO and RBAC, and formal support SLAs.
- Typical elements in Enterprise quotes: negotiated seats (or named users), usage tiers (e.g., trace ingestion/retention), hosting arrangements, annual contracts, procurement workflows, central billing, and access to an deployed engineering/support team.
