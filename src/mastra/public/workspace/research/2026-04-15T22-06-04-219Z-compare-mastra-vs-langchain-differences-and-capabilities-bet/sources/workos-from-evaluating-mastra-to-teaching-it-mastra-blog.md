## Summary

Summary:

- Case study: WorkOS adopted Mastra after discovering it on GitHub and quickly moved from evaluation to production, then to teaching it at scale within the company and at conferences.
- Real-world deployment: WorkOS engineers used Mastra to build production workflows for GTM productivity, including:
  - Automating data enrichment and formatting (unformatted text to cleaned tables; extracting companies/people; enriching and outputting structured results).
  - Web-scraping and persona filtering to produce targeted ICP-ready data (e.g., conference speaker datasets).
  - Integrating Mastra as a custom layer atop Cargo (MCP server) to select enrichment tools per workflow.
  - Easy deployment to Mastra Cloud and use of the playground for rapid testing of flows.
- Production impact: Mastra’s TypeScript-native approach and flexible workflow design resonated with WorkOS, enabling engineers to focus on business logic and deliver polished interfaces without complex UI work.
- Community engagement: WorkOS leaders (Nick Nisi and Zack Proser) ran hands-on Mastra workshops at AI Engineer World’s Fair (San Francisco) and MCP Night events at the Exploratorium, showcasing multi-step agent workflows and real-time demonstrations to hundreds of engineers, founders, and researchers.
- Takeaway: Real-world deployments highlight Mastra as a practical, flexible tool for building and teaching agent-based workflows in production, with strong emphasis on integration flexibility, rapid prototyping, and community-driven demonstrations.
