# LangSmith Plans and Pricing
- URL: https://docs.langchain.com/langsmith/pricing-plans
- Query: LangChain GitHub LICENSE and enterprise/commercial pricing or paid plans 2026
## Summary

Summary:

- Topic covered: LangSmith pricing plans (Developer, Plus, Enterprise) and related features for LangChain users.
- What you get by plan:
  - Developer (free per-seat; pay-as-you-go traces): up to 5k base traces/mo, tracing for agent debugging, evals, Prompt Hub/Playground/Canvas, annotation queues, monitoring/alerting, 1 Fleet agent, up to 50 Fleet runs/mo, community support, 1 seat.
  - Plus ($39/seat/mo; pay-as-you-go): up to 10k base traces/mo, 1 dev-sized agent deployment, email support, unlimited Fleet agents, up to 500 Fleet runs/mo (plus pay-as-you-go beyond), unlimited seats, up to 3 workspaces.
  - Enterprise (custom pricing): all Plus features plus advanced hosting options (hybrid/self-hosted), custom SSO/RBAC, access to deployed engineering team, support SLA, trainings/guidance, custom seats/workspaces, custom Fleet packages.
- Deployment and usage details:
  - Fleet: Gold-standard tracking of fleet runs; 50/mo (Dev) or 500/mo (Plus); additional runs billed per plan.
  - Tracing and ingestion: tiered base traces (5k/mo on Developer, 10k/mo on Plus) with pay-as-you-go thereafter.
  - Deployments and uptime: specified per deployment type (Dev vs Production) with per-minute uptime costs on Enterprise-like setups.
  - Observability/evaluation: includes tracing, monitoring, evals, data export, annotation queues, and tooling (Prompt Hub, Playground, etc.).
  - Language model usage billed separately via your model provider.
- Extras and discounts:
  - Small teams/VC-backed startups may get discounted rates or credits.
  - 1 free Dev deployment, unlimited deployment runs included; additional deployments priced per run.
- Notes:
  - Detailed rate limits and ingestion caps are linked in the page (rate limits).
  - Self-hosted/hybrid hosting and security features are available in Enterprise.
- For your need: If you’re evaluating pricing for 2026, the key choice is whether you need basic solo/debuggable tracing (Developer), team deployment and higher traces with more Fleet capacity (Plus), or enterprise-grade hosting/security with custom terms. If you require GitHub-style licensing (not specified here), this page does not mention GitHub licensing — it focuses on LangSmith plans.
