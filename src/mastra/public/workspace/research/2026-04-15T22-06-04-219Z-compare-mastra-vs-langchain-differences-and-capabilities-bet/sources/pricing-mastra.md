What the page covers
- Mastra pricing and deployment options for Platform (Studio and Server) and Memory Gateway.
- Three main tiers: Starter (free), Teams ($250 per team/month), and Enterprise (custom).
- Enterprise features highlighted: RBAC, dedicated support engineer, support SLAs, and on-prem/cloud deployment options.
- Memory Gateway pricing and tiers, including starter (free), Teams, and Enterprise with storage, retention, and BYOK considerations.

Key pricing highlights
- Starter (free for everyone): 
  - Studio: unlimited users/deployments; 100K observability events; 24 hours CPU uptime; 10GB data egress.
  - Memory Gateway: 100K memory tokens; 250MB retrieval storage; 15 days stale thread retention; initial $5 credit.
- Teams: 
  - Studio: unlimited users/deployments; 100K observability events; 250 hours CPU time; 100GB data egress.
  - Memory Gateway: 1M memory tokens; 1GB storage; 6 months stale thread retention; bring-your-own-key.
  - Price: $250 per team/month.
- Enterprise (custom pricing):
  - Both Platform and Memory Gateway include RBAC, dedicated support engineer, support SLA, and customizable CPU time/storage/egress.
  - On-prem/cloud deployment options; tailored data retention and storage configurations.

Enterprise support offerings
- Agent Lifecycle Support: planning, rapid prototyping, productionizing, and hands-on training.
- Collaboration & SLAs: architecture/design meetings, shared Slack support, separate SLAs, standard business hours support.
- On-prem deployments: Mastra platform on-prem or in customer cloud, SSO and RBAC, SRE support during business hours.

What this means for you (practical guidance)
- If you’re evaluating Mastra for a small team or trial, Starter is free with generous limits on users and deployments, plus basic observability and data egress.
- If you need multi-team access, more CPU time, larger data egress, or enterprise-grade security/compliance (RBAC, SOC 2 docs) and dedicated support, Teams or Enterprise would be the better fit.
- For large organizations needing custom SLAs, on-prem deployments, or BYOK and advanced retention/storage controls, contact Mastra for Enterprise pricing.
- If you specifically require ongoing enterprise support, SLA-backed services, or on-prem deployment details, Enterprise Support offerings outline the scope and availability windows.
