- Query: LangChain GitHub LICENSE and enterprise/commercial pricing or paid plans 2026
## Summary

Summary:
- LangChain’s LangSmith self-hosting is available only via the Enterprise plan.
- Enterprise plan includes: unlimited seats, enterprise support, a shared Slack channel, and access to Helm charts for deployment.
- To obtain a self-hosted license key: contact LangChain Sales to discuss Enterprise licensing, complete the enterprise contract and payment process, then receive a license key.
- Deployment is via official Helm charts: https://github.com/langchain-ai/helm/releases
- The article implies licensing and deployment are tied to the Enterprise offering; there is no publicly accessible, standalone GitHub LICENSE for self-hosted usage outside Enterprise. For 2026 pricing/details, contact sales for current Enterprise pricing and terms.
