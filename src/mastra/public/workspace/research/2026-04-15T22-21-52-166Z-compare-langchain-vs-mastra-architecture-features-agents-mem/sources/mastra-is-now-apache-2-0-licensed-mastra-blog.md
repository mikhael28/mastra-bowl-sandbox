## Summary

Summary:

- Mastra has transitioned to the Apache 2.0 license, moving from ELv2 to a more permissive license to reduce friction for adoption and contributions.
- Apache 2.0 permits commercial use, modification, distribution (original or modified), and private use, with the requirement to include the original license text.
- The change addresses inquiries from larger organizations seeking a permissive license while preserving openness and community collaboration.
- The announcement emphasizes the benefits of open source for innovation and collaboration, with a brief note from Sam Bhagwat (CEO).
