## Summary

Summary:

- Maestro Labs emphasizes built-in privacy and security across its platform, with multiple compliance attestations:
  - GDPR Compliant
  - SOC 2 Type II Compliant
  - HIPAA Compliant (with HIPAA-aligned controls and BAA options)
  - Microsoft Attested; Google Verified; CASA Tier 2 Verified
- Data handling and minimisation:
  - PHI-handling controls and BAAs available on request
  - Data only collected as needed; not used for advertising or AI training
  - Privacy by Design and Default
  - Authorized sub-processors under contractual safeguards
- Data protection measures:
  - HIPAA-aligned safeguards, audit logging, access monitoring
  - Regular security audits, penetration testing, MFA, least-privilege access
  - Data encrypted at rest and in transit
- International transfers and residency:
  - BAAs for PHI sub-processors; SCCs for international transfers
  - GDPR-aligned with other privacy laws; Trust Center for sub-processor list and TIAs
- Rights, controls, and retention:
  - HIPAA customers can obtain or customize BAAs; rights requests handled via support
  - Access, correction, deletion, restriction available
  - PHI retention per HIPAA and BAA; zero-day retention for enterprise; data retained only as long as needed
- Incident response:
  - HIPAA breach notification procedures; GDPR-aligned breach assessment and notifications
  - Comprehensive incident response plan
- Resources:
  - Trust Center available for detailed security posture, attestations, and policies
