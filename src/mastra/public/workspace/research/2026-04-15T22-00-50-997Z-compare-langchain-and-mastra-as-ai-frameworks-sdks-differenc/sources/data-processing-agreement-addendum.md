# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT ADDENDUM

## Summary

Summary:

- The document is a Data Processing Agreement Addendum (DPA) from Maistra, to be attached as a schedule to a Master Agreement.
- It governs the processing of personal data and Personally Identifiable Information (PII) exchanged between the parties in the context of providing services to guests and employees.
- Legal framework referenced:
  - GDPR (EU 2016/679)
  - Croatian data protection laws and implementing acts
  - Applicable EU and local privacy regulations
- Roles and responsibilities:
  - Each party acts as an independent data controller, determining means and purposes of processing within their own business operations.
  - If a party becomes a processor on instruction of the other, a separate data processing agreement under Article 28 GDPR will be required.
- Processing scope and purposes:
  - Personal data is shared only for Agreed Purposes tied to providing accommodation and ancillary services under the Master Agreement.
  - Sharing methods include email (reservation lists), the PHOBS online reservation system (integrated with Maistra Extranet), and direct reservations center contact for corporate agreements.
- Notable terms:
  - The document defines standard data protection terms (controller/processor, data subject, etc.) per GDPR.
  - Annex or addendum structure to be applied alongside the Master Agreement.

Important for enterprise readers:
- If you require a HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA) in addition to this DPA, this Croatian GDPR-based DPA does not address HIPAA/BAA terms, and you would need a separate BAA aligned with HIPAA requirements. This page references GDPR and Croatian laws only, not HIPAA.
- For 2026 enterprise deployments, verify whether you need:
  - A HIPAA-compliant BAA with Maistra (if handling protected health information in the U.S. context or with covered entities/business associates)
  - Any updated privacy clauses reflecting changes in GDPR/CGPR/Croatian amendments and cross-border data transfer mechanisms (SCCs, UKADD, etc.)
  - A formal Data Processing Addendum detailing subprocessors, security measures, data subject rights handling, breach notification timelines, and audit rights.
