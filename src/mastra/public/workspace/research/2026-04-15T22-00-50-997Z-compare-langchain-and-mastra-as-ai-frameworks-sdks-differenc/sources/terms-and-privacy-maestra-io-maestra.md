## Summary

Summary:

- The page covers Maestra’s Terms and Privacy, including security, data handling, compliance, and data residency. It is not an SLA document, so direct SLA details ( uptime, response times, crediting, penalties, support SLAs, etc.) are not specified here.

Key points relevant to an enterprise SLA request:
- Security and compliance:
  - SOC 2 Type II certified (2025) and GDPR/CCPA compliant; HIPAA-friendly with BAAs available on request.
  - Password protections, 2FA, RBAC, audit logs, data masking, SIEM support.
- Data ownership and privacy:
  - Customers maintain ownership; immutable consent logs; supports GDPR right-to-delete workflows; automatic Shopify deletion sync; auditable processing.
- Data residency and governance:
  - Data stored in isolated environments per company; first-party data only; data can be isolated for multi-brand/multi-region setups.
  - Customer data stored on AWS in the United States; governing law: Delaware law (US).
- SMS and regional compliance:
  - Texas SMS laws and other regional regulations; double opt-in; carrier-supported compliance.
- Third-party sharing:
  - No sale or sharing of data beyond stated purposes; first-party data only.

What’s missing for a formal SLA:
- Specific uptime targets (e.g., 99.9%), MTTR, response/resolution times by severity, and credit/penalty terms.
- Detailed support SLAs (business hours vs 24/7, response times by issue type, escalation paths).
- Data residency specifics beyond generic US storage (availability of regional data centers, cross-region replication, failover SLAs).
- Disaster recovery objectives (RPO/RTO) and backup frequency.
- Change management, maintenance windows, and notification protocols.
- Penalties, service credits, and termination/exit terms.

Recommendation:
- If you need an enterprise SLA, request a formal SLA addendum or a dedicated enterprise term sheet from Maestra that includes uptime targets, support SLAs, data residency options, DR/BCP details, and credits. You can reference the security/compliance guarantees in this document as supporting controls.
