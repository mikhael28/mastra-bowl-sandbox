## Summary

Summary:

- LangSmith Enterprise is a scalable, secure observability and debugging platform for LangChain, optimized for production-grade AI agent applications with strict governance, data residency, and compliance needs.
- Billing and usage: annual invoiced plan with named-user licensing (per seat) and trace-based metering. Enterprise can pre-purchase LangSmith Credits with alerts and auto top-ups to prevent disruptions.
- Deployment and compliance:
  - Self-hosted option: deploy in your own Kubernetes environment on AWS/GCP/Azure, ensuring full infra control, data residency, and no data leaving your VPC.
  - Managed Private Cloud (optional): LangChain handles deployment in your private cloud.
  - Certifications: SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, GDPR; BAAs available; 100% data ownership with no training on customer data.
- Administration and governance:
  - SSO integration, RBAC, custom SLAs, and API rate controls to manage access, usage, and performance.
- White-glove support:
  - Dedicated Customer Success Engineer, dedicated Slack channel, monthly strategic check-ins, and infrastructure support for deployments and upgrades.
